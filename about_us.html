About Us

Company Info

Anthony's Travel Agency was founded originally in 1943, when a group of Continental Airlines Employees left to found their own travel company. It all started with about 6 guys and no planes, but has since expanded to a team of hundreds with locations around the continental United States, with a collection of private jet-liner aircraft.
